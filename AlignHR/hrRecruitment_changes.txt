Business Requirement Clarification – Recruitment Department Approval Structure

Please implement the following recruitment ownership model.

==================================================
Recruitment Department Structure
================================

The organization contains multiple business departments.

Example:

* IT
* Infrastructure
* Security
* Finance
* HR
* Sales
* Operations

Multiple business departments can belong to a single Recruitment Department.

Example:

Technology Recruitment Department
- IT
- Infrastructure
- Security
- Data

Corporate Recruitment Department
- Finance
- HR
- Legal

Commercial Recruitment Department
- Sales
- Marketing
- Operations

==================================================
Recruitment Department Ownership
================================

Each Recruitment Department has one designated Recruitment Head.

Example:

Technology Recruitment Department
Recruitment Head = Ali

Corporate Recruitment Department
Recruitment Head = Hassan

Commercial Recruitment Department
Recruitment Head = Bilal

==================================================
Workflow Requirement
====================

After the Line Manager, HRBP, and HOD approvals are completed, the requisition must move to the Recruitment Head responsible for the department associated with that requisition.

Example:

Department = IT

IT belongs to Technology Recruitment Department.

Technology Recruitment Department Head = Ali.

Therefore, only Ali should receive this requisition for recruitment approval.

==================================================
Recruitment Head Responsibilities
=================================

The Recruitment Head must:

1. Review the approved requisition.
2. Approve or reject the recruitment request from the recruitment perspective.
3. Assign the requisition to a recruiter within their own recruitment team.

==================================================
Visibility Rules
================

* Recruitment Heads should only see requisitions belonging to departments assigned to their Recruitment Department.
* Recruitment Heads must not see requisitions outside their assigned departments.
* Recruiters should only see requisitions assigned directly to them.
* Recruiters cannot assign or reassign requisitions.
* Only the Recruitment Head can perform recruiter assignment.

==================================================
Example Flow
============

Line Manager
↓
Department HRBP
↓
Department HOD
↓
Recruitment Head Approval
↓
Recruiter Assignment
↓
Recruitment Activities Begin

Example:

IT Department Request
↓
HRBP (IT)
↓
HOD (IT)
↓
Technology Recruitment Head (Ali)
↓
Assigned to Recruiter (Hamza)

Finance Department Request
↓
HRBP (Finance)
↓
HOD (Finance)
↓
Corporate Recruitment Head (Hassan)
↓
Assigned to Recruiter (Saad)

==================================================
Expected Outcome
================

The system must support many departments being managed by a single Recruitment Department while maintaining strict ownership and visibility controls.

Approval and assignment must always be determined by department-to-recruitment-department mapping rather than generic RBAC role matching.
